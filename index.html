Charlottesville

Charlottesville is many things to many people: Jefferson-town, home of the Hoos, the nation's fastest-growing tech hub, and now? Virginia's design destination.

For three years, hundreds of the commonwealth's designers have convened in Cville for a week of mixing, learning, sharing, and showing off. The Charlottesville Design Week, which is almost completely free to attend, has two simple goals:

  1. Help Virginia's designers develop a strong sense of community
  2. Convince the general public that good design is worth a damn

Whether you push pixels, press ink, set type, build environments, sew dresses, or do basically anything else design-related, you will feel right at home during our growing festival.

Week

Tentative schedule for the fourth Charlottesville Design Week happening in April 2023.

    Tuesday the 4th

  • Opening Meetup

    Kick off the festivities with Tuesday Design Society's weekly meetup. Introduce yourself, bring a design to show off, and get to know the people behind the Charlottesville Design Week.

    Wednesday the 5th

  • Firm Crawl

    Take a guided tour through Charlottesville's downtown design firms. Visit the agencies’ offices, hear about their work, and see the available creative opportunities. Bring your resume!

  • Portfolio Review

    Present your portfolio to a panel of expert judges... in front of a crowd of peers. Don't worry, though, it's more Great British Baking Show than American Idol. Who knows? You might even leave with a job.

  • Trivia Night

    Your friendly trivia night but with a design twist. Design nerds assemble into randomized teams to answer design-centered trivia. The prize—it's the friends you make along the way, right? Beyond new friendships, who knows what trivia champions will win!

    Thursday the 6th

  • Design Marathon

    Donate a day of your creative juices to a deserving local non-profit. Participants form several “agencies-for-a-day,” working with a variety of designers, copywriters, and developers to satisfy the beneficiary's brief.

  • Marathon Showcase

    See the Design Marathon agencies present their work to the non-profit. It's like the best scenes from Mad Men, but live and in person. Not to mention, the selected design will be put to use by the non-profit for years to come.
