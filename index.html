Charlottesville

Charlottesville is many things to many people: Jefferson-town, home of the Hoos, the nation's fastest-growing tech hub, and now? Virginia's design destination.

For three years, hundreds of the commonwealth's designers have convened in Cville for a week of connecting, learning, sharing, and showcasing local talent. The Charlottesville Design Week is 100% volunteer-led and has two simple goals:

  1. Help designers develop a strong sense of community in central Virginia
  2. Convince the general public that good design is worth a damn

Whether you push pixels, press ink, set type, build environments, or do anything else design-related, you will feel right at home during our growing festival.

Week

Event schedule for the fourth Charlottesville Design Week happening in April 2023.

    Tuesday the 4th

  • Opening Meetup

    Kick off the festivities with Tuesday Design Society's weekly meetup. Introduce yourself, bring a design to show off, and get to know other local designers and creatives.

    Wednesday the 5th

  • Firm Crawl

    Take a guided tour through Charlottesville's downtown design firms. Visit the agencies’ offices, hear about their work, and see the available creative opportunities.

  • Portfolio Review

    Present your portfolio to a panel of expert judges... in front of a crowd of peers. Don't worry, though, it's more Great British Baking Show than American Idol.

    Thursday the 6th

  • Design Marathon

    Donate a day of your creative juices to a deserving local non-profit. Participants form several “agencies-for-a-day,” working with a variety of designers, copywriters, and developers to satisfy the beneficiary's brief.

  • Marathon Showcase

    See the Design Marathon agencies present their work to the non-profit. It's like the best scenes from Mad Men, but live and in person. Not to mention, the selected design will be put to use by the non-profit for years to come.

  • Film Screening

    Help yourself to some popcorn—we’re projecting a documentary on a design-related topic onto a large screen in front of some really comfy chairs.

    Friday the 7th

  • The Forum

    A day-long conference featuring local and national design leaders, examining a cross section of design practices.

  • Reception

    Mix and mingle with speakers from the Forum and fellow attendees.

    Saturday the 8th

  • Closing Party

    Say goodbye to the fourth Charlottesville Design Week by spending an evening with all the new friends you made at it!

    All Week

  • Workshop Series

    Learn a thing or two! We have workshops covering light photography, design thinking practices, letterpress printing, and more!
